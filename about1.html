FOUNDER OF SIVAPARVATHI RICE MILL

Our Story:

SivaParvathi Rice Mill, founded by Cherukuri Devarajulu in 2014, is a prominent and successful rice mill located in the heart of South India, specifically in Andhra Pradesh. The mill operates around the clock, 24/7, and has established itself as a leading player in the rice industry in India.

Key points about SivaParvathi Rice Mill:

1. **Founder and Establishment**:

Cherukuri Devarajulu initiated the mill's operations in 2014, and it has been running successfully ever since.

2. **Integrated Approach**:

SivaParvathi Rice Mill takes a health-centric approach and is fully integrated from the cultivation of rice to the end customer. This integration ensures the highest quality products are delivered directly from the grower to the consumer.

3. **Diverse Markets**:

The mill serves various markets, including foodservice, retail, export, and ingredients markets, which demonstrates its versatility and ability to cater to a wide range of customers.

4. **Rice-Producing Region**:

The mill is strategically located in Andhra Pradesh, a leading rice-producing state in India. This location provides access to abundant rice crops and contributes to the mill's success.

5. **Farmer Collaboration**:

SivaParvathi Rice Mill collaborates with over 500 farmer members who are an integral part of the ShivaParvathi family. The mill values the dedication and care that these rice producers invest in their rice crops and land.

6. **Regional Presence**:

While primarily operating in Andhra Pradesh, SivaParvathi Rice Mill also extends its operations to Tamil Nadu, which further solidifies its presence in the South Indian rice market.

7. **Proud Name**:

The name "SivaParvathi Rice Mill" is chosen with pride, signifying the mill's respect for its farmers, known as "rice producers," and their commitment to rice cultivation.

Overall, SivaParvathi Rice Mill appears to be a well-established and reputable rice processing facility that places great emphasis on quality, integration, and collaboration with local farmers. Its dedication to delivering high-quality rice products to various markets has contributed to its ongoing success in the industry.
